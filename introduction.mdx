# Welcome to ESBO Web

ESBO Web is a modern, responsive landing page built for **Escuela Sistémica Boliviana** (ESBO) - a pioneering institution in Bolivia that combines a mental health clinic (**Clínica Sistémica de la Familia**) with high-quality educational programs in systemic therapy and family counseling.

## What is ESBO?

ESBO (Escuela Sistémica Boliviana) is a unique institution that offers:

- **Mental Health Services**: Professional therapy and family counseling through Clínica Sistémica de la Familia
- **Educational Programs**: Diplomas, courses, and certifications in systemic therapy
- **International Partnerships**: Collaborations with global institutions for academic validation
- **Academic Resources**: A comprehensive repository of research and educational materials

## Landing Page Sections

The ESBO Web landing page includes carefully designed sections:

### Hero Section
A striking dual-image hero with overlay featuring the SINTEGRA tagline: "Salud Mental y Formación de Excelencia" (Mental Health and Educational Excellence). Includes prominent call-to-action buttons for scheduling appointments and exploring educational programs.

### Service Highlights
Three key institutional features presented with elegant iconography:
- **Academic Repository**: Access to research and educational materials
- **International Partnerships**: Global academic collaborations
- **Certificate Validation**: Official certification verification system

### Contact & Social Media
Comprehensive contact section featuring:
- Multiple email addresses for different departments
- Phone contact with Bolivia country code
- Facebook pages for both the school and clinic
- Instagram presence for community engagement

### Interactive Elements
- Student/Staff access portal button
- Floating chat widget for immediate support
- Search functionality for finding information quickly
- FAQ section for common clinic questions
